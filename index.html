About us

Chitkara Esports Club is an official Chitkara University club for Esports. We at Chitkara Esports believe in Quality over quantity and here you will get the best tournaments Experience in Competetive Environment, buckle up your seat belts and stay tuned with us for this excitng journey. The purpose of this website is to provide a platform for members to interact with other members, admins of CEC and our gaming community spread all over the nation. This platform also serves as a means for us to host various other online tournaments and events.

What we do?


-Our club is dedicated to host Esports events and tournaments as the name suggests.
