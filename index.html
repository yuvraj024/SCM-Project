About us

Chitkara Esports Club is an official Chitkara University club forn Esports. We at Chitkara Esports believe in Quality over quantity and here you will get the best tournaments Experience in Competetive Environment, buckle up your seat belts and stay tuned with us for this excitng journey. The purpose of this website is to provide a platform for members to interact with other members, admins of CEC and our gaming community spread all over the nation. This platform also serves as a means for us to host various other online tournaments and events.

What we do?

Our club is dedicated to host Esports events and tournaments as the name suggests.

What games do we host?

The events are community driven. We will be hosting events for those games that the community demands for, as of now according to the survey we took, we have received responses for the following games -

  1. Valorant
  2. CS:GO
  3. BGMI
  4. Apex Legends
  5. Rocket League
  6. Fortnite
  7. Fifa
  8. Warzone
  9. Dota 2
  10. Brawlhalla

How to become a member?

To become a member you have to register through chalkpad > Registration Forms > Chitkara ESports Club

What are the perks of becoming a member?

Members will be eligible for the following benefits
  1. Discounted fee for event registration
  2. Member only events
  3. Eligible for joining the team (edited)
  4. November 26, 2021
